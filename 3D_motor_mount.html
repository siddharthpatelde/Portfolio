3D-Printed-Motor-Mount

Situation

In the company, my work primarily involved electronics and programming. My initial task was to create a circuit/logic for a differential drive robot and later program it. However, before I could begin with that, I needed to attach the motors to the body provided by my professor.

My current assignment required me to design and 3D print a motor mount to secure the motors onto the chassis. Despite lacking prior experience in 3D printing, this task became a crucial step in the project


Task

1.Quickly grasp Fusion 360 CAD software.

2.Obtain essential measurements to initiate the motor mount design.

3.Always refer to the motor's dimension sheet for accuracy.

3.Once the correct 3D design is achieved, proceed to print it and attach it securely to the robot body.
